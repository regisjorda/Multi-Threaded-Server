The purpose of this course to teach students the fundamentals of playing sports students will engage in sports activities as well as learn the history of it. The main sports that will be covered are listed below.

Hockey
Is a family of sports in which two teams play against each other by trying to maneuver a ball or a puck into the opponent's goal using a hockey stick.
Basketball
Is a team sport, the objective being to shoot a ball through a basket horizontally positioned to score points while following a set of rules.
Baseball
Is a bat-and-ball sport played between two teams of nine players. The aim is to score runs by hitting a thrown ball with a bat and touching a series of four bases arranged at the corners of a 90-foot diamond.
Soccer
Is a sport played between two teams of eleven players with a spherical ball.
Volleyball
Is a team sport in which two teams of six players are separated by a net.
Football
Is a sport played between two teams of eleven with the objective of scoring points by advancing the ball into the opposing team's end zone by running with it or throwing it to a teammate.
